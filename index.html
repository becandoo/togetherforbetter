The Cole Family, Together for Better

In 2011, The Cole Family decided to begin a new Christmas tradition. Little did they know that their small act of kindness, would turn into a local phenomenon. Giving proof that a small act of kindness will change lives, spark a movement, and grow into something epic.

Every morning, on December 25th, the Cole Family wakes up, gather the donations they have collected over the past months and heads into downtown Portland to begin showing the homeless that people care. They spend hours putting smiles on the faces of those that they have never met.

From the seed of that new Christmas tradition, other traditions have formed, from helping out at local foodbanks, donating time to other charities, and working to make the world a better place, one smile at at a time.
